## Notes for the writer

- Post follows the 8-part structure: hook, problem, perspective shift, partial solution, limitation, soft product bridge, CTA, closing.
- Tone is calm, grounded, and human. No hype. No aggressive selling.
- No em or en dashes in the post body.
- Two internal links per post: Creator Guides hub and the specific guide.
- The Freedom Library (/reading-guide) is a separate product line. Only reference it if the topic is about sovereignty or the freedom stack.
- Bundle mention is one line max, mirroring the "Also in ... System" pattern from Creator Guides.
